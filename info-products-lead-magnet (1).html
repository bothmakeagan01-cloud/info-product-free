Mistake #2: Weak Follow-Up

The One-Size-Fits-None Problem

Sending the same emails to everyone is like using a shotgun when you need a sniper rifle.

Signs Your Follow-Up Is Broken

  • Open rates below 20%
  • Click rates below 2%
  • Same sequence for quiz takers and webinar attendees
  • No behavioral triggers
  • Generic "newsletter" content

Mistake #3: No Upsell/Downsell System

Leaving Money on the Table

If you're not offering upsells and downsells, you're literally refusing money from buyers with their credit cards out.

The Hidden Cost of No Upsells

  • Average order value stuck at initial price
  • Missing 20-30% additional revenue per sale
  • Higher CAC with no backend to offset
  • Competitors offering more value at checkout

The Fix: Strategic Value Stack

Core Offer - $497
Upsell 1: Advanced Training - $297
Upsell 2: 1-on-1 Call - $197
Downsell: Payment Plan - $97/mo

The Perfect Upsell Formula

  1. Complementary: Enhances the core offer
  2. Exclusive: Only available at checkout
  3. Discounted: 30-50% off "regular" price
  4. One-Click: No re-entering payment info
  5. Risk-Free: Same guarantee as core offer
